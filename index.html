About Me
About me

UI/UX Designer & Developer

Where design
meets code.

I'm a UI/UX designer and developer who lives at the intersection of aesthetics and engineering. My background in Computer Science & Engineering gives me a rare edge — I don't just design beautiful things, I build them.

I craft interaction-rich, user-centered websites — with deep focus on usability, micro-animations, and experiences that feel alive from first click to last scroll.

"The combination of my passion for design, code & interaction positions me in a unique place in the web design world."

My UI/UX process

01
Discover
Research users, goals, and pain points before touching any tool.
02
Wireframe
Low-fi layouts to map structure, hierarchy, and user flows.
03
Design
High-fidelity Figma prototypes with full design system components.
04
Build
Pixel-perfect development in Webflow with smooth interactions.

Design vs development

UI/UX Design
User research, wireframing, prototyping, design systems, visual hierarchy, accessibility, and interaction design — all grounded in real user needs.
Development
Scalable Webflow builds, micro-animations, responsive layouts, and clean code — bridging the gap between design intent and live product.
